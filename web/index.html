How To Play

1

Start a New Game

Launch the app and tap on the "Start New Game" button to begin your movie guessing challenge. Each game consists of multiple rounds of increasing difficulty.

2

Watch the GIF

A short GIF clip from a movie will be displayed. Watch carefully for any clues that might help you identify the film - actors, scenes, settings, or iconic moments.

3

Choose Your Answer

Select from six possible movie titles. Only one is correct! Choose wisely to earn points and build your streak. The faster you answer, the more points you'll earn.

4

Build Your Score

Each correct answer earns you points and increases your streak multiplier. Try to answer all questions correctly to maximize your score and climb the leaderboard!
