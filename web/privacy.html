Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

Welcome to ClipCryptic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our movie GIF quiz game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User-Generated Information: When you create an account, we generate a unique user identifier that helps us track your game progress, scores, and achievements.
  • Game Activity: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including your game scores, answer selections, completion rates, and time spent playing.
  • Device Information: We automatically collect certain information about your device, including your mobile device ID, model and manufacturer, operating system, and screen resolution.
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information that your device sends whenever you use our app, such as your IP address, app crashes, and system activity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Track your progress and display your scores on leaderboards
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and ensure app stability
  • Communicate with you about updates, new features, or promotional offers (if you opt in)

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely on our servers and on your local device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, or destruction.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share non-personally identifiable, aggregated information for analytics purposes. Your username and scores may be visible to other users on leaderboards, but this can be adjusted in your privacy settings.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of appearing on public leaderboards through the app settings
  • Delete your user data from our servers through the settings menu
  • Disable notifications in your device settings or within the app

Children's Privacy

ClipCryptic is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

privacy@clipcryptic.com
